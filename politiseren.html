kabk studentenvakbond [2023 - 2024 / voortdurend]

Peyman Fazeli, Omid Kheirabadi, Sonya Umanskaya, Alina Lupu and Pum van de Koppel founded the kabk student union to advocate for:

  • a bigger influence of students in managerial decisionmaking
  • transparency from the boards and the managers
  • making the KABK a radically inclusive structure
  • a practical approach to political education

The kabk student union originated as a response to a longer history of mismanagement and misconduct in the academy and also aims to archive the struggles and topics that students bring forth, to function as a counterweight to the transient nature of (graduating) students and teachers without permanent contracts.

kabk student union has a pragmatic approach which is illustrated by an actively maintained conversation channel with the management and thinks DIY solutions are necessary in a slow and old institution such as the KABK.

Pum van de Koppel is no longer studying and is proud of the next generation of students taking up the union, but believes that the poetic power of art and imaginative politics are closely related and that they continually inform each other.
