As a Technical University dropout and an Art Academy alumnus, Pum van de Koppel is here to make your new website, with the artistic experience needed to think through creative choices, categorization and the aesthetic experience together with the client.

The construction of a website consists of the following elements:

Als dropout van de Technische Universiteit en alumnus van de Kunstacademie, staat Pum van de Koppel klaar om je nieuwe website te maken, met de benodigde artistieke ervaring om samen met de klant mee te denken in creatieve keuzes, categorisatie en aesthetisch gevoel.

De totstandkoming van een website bestaat uit:

  1. (non-binding) introductory meeting
    • A collecting of inspirations and general ideas for the website. A first sketch for the wireframe is being constructed together.

  2. the making of the website
    • After an agreement on price, deadline and advance payment, the client sends over all the visual and textual material and Pum van de Koppel starts working on the website with an open line of communication.

  3. checking in and wrapping up
    • After about 2/3 of the website is done, another meeting is planned to check the direction of the website. Afterwards Pum van de Koppel finishes the website and helps the client with configuring their chosen domain.
