Five from the archive: 'self' [2019-2024]

Vijf uit het archief: 'zelf' [2019-2024]


Drawing remains at the heart of Pum van de Koppel's artistic practice throughout the years and has provided him an important outlet in times of turmoil.
To free the accumulated drawings from their weighty storage space Pum van de Koppel is starting a new series called Five from the archive where he picks five drawings which surround a common theme to reproduce in print.

The theme that emerged for the first edition is 'self'.
The drawings are printed on 300 g/m² paper in an edition of twenty-five and come in a set of five for €25. The prints are signed and numbered.
